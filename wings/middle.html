About This Wing

Middle Wing

Middle Wing is more than a school — it is a second home for our students. Established as an autonomous wing in 2017, it caters to Classes VI, VII, and VIII, providing a nurturing bridge between primary education and the more demanding years of secondary schooling.

We believe in the holistic development of every child. Beyond academics, we focus on character building, emotional growth, creativity, and social responsibility. Through a nurturing environment and dedicated faculty, we shape confident, curious, and compassionate individuals ready to thrive in all aspects of life.

At Middle Wing, we don't just teach — we grow together. Our approach combines structured academics with meaningful co-curricular experiences, ensuring that each student leaves not only with knowledge but with the values, resilience, and curiosity that will serve them for life.

Message from the Vice Principal

Ms. Saadia Khanam Vice Principal, Middle Wing

The middle school years are among the most critical in a child's development — a period of intellectual curiosity, emotional growth, and the gradual shaping of identity. At Middle Wing, we take that responsibility seriously, providing every student with a safe, stimulating, and structured environment where they are truly seen, heard, and encouraged to flourish.

Our dedicated faculty are committed not only to academic excellence but to the holistic development of each child. We nurture confidence, critical thinking, creativity, and compassion — building the foundation upon which all future learning and achievement rests. Together, we grow as a community of learners and leaders.

Our Strengths

Why Middle Wing?

Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we invest in every child's emotional intelligence, creativity, physical wellbeing, and social responsibility — shaping well-rounded individuals ready for all aspects of life.

Dedicated Faculty

Our experienced and caring teaching staff understand the unique needs of middle-school learners. They provide personalised attention, consistent mentorship, and an environment where every student feels valued.

Character Building

Rooted in Pakistan Navy's values of integrity, discipline, and service, we cultivate strong moral character alongside academic knowledge — preparing students to be responsible citizens and future leaders.

Academic Foundation

Classes VI–VIII are the critical bridge to secondary education. Our rigorous yet engaging curriculum ensures every student builds the conceptual depth, study habits, and intellectual confidence needed to excel in higher classes.
