About This Wing

Girls Wing (Morning)

Girls Wing was established in March 2002. The Wing is affiliated with Federal Board at the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) & Higher Secondary School levels and also with the Karachi Board at the Intermediate level.

The Girls Wing offers a diverse range of academic disciplines. At the Secondary level (Class IX), students can opt for Biology, Computer Science, and Humanities with subjects such as Economics and Food & Nutrition. At the Higher Secondary level (Class XI), the available streams include Pre-Medical, Pre-Engineering, General Science, Commerce, and Humanities (Federal Board) offering subjects Psychology, Economics, English elective etc.

Girls Wing has a strength of over 900 students, supported by a dedicated faculty of more than 55 members across various departments.

Message from the Vice Principal

Ms. Farzana Faisal Vice Principal, Girls Wing (Morning)

Leading the Girls Wing (Morning) is both a privilege and a responsibility we take with the utmost dedication. Our wing has grown into a centre of academic excellence since its establishment in 2002, and we remain firmly committed to empowering our students with knowledge, discipline, and the confidence to excel in every walk of life.

We believe in holistic development — nurturing not only academic ability but also character, resilience, and leadership. Our faculty works tirelessly to create an environment where each student feels supported, motivated, and inspired to achieve her fullest potential, both in board examinations and in the years that follow.

Academic Streams

Programmes Offered

Six distinct SSC and HSSC programmes under Federal Board and Karachi Board, designed to prepare students for top universities.

Pre-Medical

Biology, Chemistry, Physics & English — pathway to MBBS, BDS and health sciences.

Pre-Engineering

Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry & English — foundation for engineering, architecture and technology degrees.

Computer Science

CS, Mathematics, Physics & English — gateway to IT, software engineering and data science.

Commerce

Accounting, Business Studies, Economics & English — preparation for BBA, CA, ACCA and finance careers.

General Science

A broad science-based programme under the Federal Board for students exploring diverse scientific disciplines.

Humanities

Psychology, Economics, English Elective & more — opening pathways to social sciences, law, and liberal arts.

Examination Authority

Affiliated Boards

Board of Intermediate Education, Karachi (BIEK) & Federal Board

The Girls Wing (Morning) is affiliated with both the Board of Intermediate Education, Karachi (BIEK) for Intermediate-level examinations and the Federal Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (FBISE) for SSC and HSSC levels. This dual affiliation gives students the flexibility to pursue their preferred examination pathway. Both boards uphold rigorous academic standards, ensuring BCKz students are fully prepared for higher education and competitive entry tests.
