Pakistan Navy Recognition

Award of Medals

Pakistan Navy honours the top board position holders from Bahria College Karsaz with prestigious gold medals and cash prizes, recognising extraordinary academic performance in SSC and HSSC examinations.

🏋

CNS Gold Medal

Rs 35,000

Awarded to the student securing 1st position in SSC or HSSC Board Examination, presented by the Chief of Naval Staff.

🥈

Chairman BOG Gold Medal

Rs 25,000

Institutional Excellence

Best Bahria College Award

Bahria College Karsaz has been honoured as the Best Bahria College for 12 years — specifically for the years 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2024, and 2025. This distinguished award is conferred by Pakistan Navy's Education Directorate and recognises the college that demonstrates the highest standards of academic achievement, institutional discipline, co-curricular excellence, and overall management across the entire Bahria Colleges network nationwide.

This unmatched track record reflects the sustained commitment of the faculty, administration, and student body to uphold BCKz's founding principles of excellence, integrity, and service — making it the benchmark institution among all Bahria Colleges in Pakistan.
