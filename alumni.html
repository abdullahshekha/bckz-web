Our Graduates

Our Alumni Community

Since its founding in 1997, Bahria College Karsaz has been the launching pad for thousands of young Pakistanis who have gone on to make their mark in every field imaginable. From the operating theatres of leading hospitals to the engineering floors of multinational corporations, BCKz graduates carry with them the values, discipline, and intellectual rigour that define a BCKz education.

Our alumni have distinguished themselves in the Pakistan Armed Forces, the judiciary, the civil services, and the world of business and finance. Many have pursued postgraduate studies at prestigious universities both within Pakistan and abroad, returning with the knowledge and drive to contribute meaningfully to their communities and professions.

The BCKz alumni community spans over two decades and continues to grow. Whether you graduated in the early 2000s or walked the stage last year, you are a permanent part of this institution's story — and we are proud to count you among our own. We invite all former students to stay connected, give back, and help inspire the generation that follows.

Career Paths

Where Our Alumni Excel

BCKz graduates have built successful careers across every major sector — a testament to the breadth and depth of the education they received here.

Medicine & Healthcare

BCKz's Pre-Medical programme has propelled hundreds of graduates into MBBS, BDS, and allied health sciences programmes at Pakistan's top medical colleges.

Engineering & Technology

From civil and mechanical to software and electrical engineering, our alumni are shaping Pakistan's infrastructure, tech startups, and global technology firms.

Pakistan Armed Forces

Rooted in the Navy's tradition of service, many BCKz alumni have gone on to serve with distinction in the Pakistan Army, Navy, and Air Force.

Law & Judiciary

Alumni from our Humanities stream have pursued careers in law, the civil service, and the judiciary — upholding justice and public service across the country.

Business & Finance

Our Commerce graduates lead organisations, manage portfolios, and run enterprises — contributing to Pakistan's economic growth both at home and internationally.

Education & Research

Many alumni have chosen to give back through education and research — joining universities, think tanks, and research institutions to advance knowledge in their fields.
